Wunderground - An underground art festival
To celebrate 40 years of metro in Brussels.
Previous Next
Client: MIVB/STIB
Role: Design & Branding
Deliverables: Branding, Design, Promo
To celebrate the 40th birthday of the Brussels' metro system, and following the unfortunate events of March 2016, MIVB/STIB decided to put in place an underground art festival, dedicated to Regaining the historical level of attendance in the underground stations & Improving their relationship with young professionals in Brussels.
